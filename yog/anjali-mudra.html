Anjali Mudra

  • 1 Sit or stand tall with your heart open.
  • 2 Slowly bring your palms together in front of your chest.
  • 3 Gently press your fingers and palms against each other.
  • 4 Rest your thumbs lightly against your sternum.
  • 5 Breathe deeply, connecting your mind and heart.

Anjali Mudra is a gesture of reverence and benediction. It harmonizes the two hemispheres of the brain and brings your focus back to the heart center, promoting a sense of inner peace and equilibrium.
