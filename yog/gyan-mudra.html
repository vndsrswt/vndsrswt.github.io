Gyan Mudra

  • 1 Sit in a comfortable pose like Padmasana.
  • 2 Rest your hands on your knees, palms up.
  • 3 Touch the tip of your index finger to your thumb.
  • 4 Keep the other three fingers straight and relaxed.
  • 5 Hold the gesture and observe your calm mind.

Gyan Mudra represents the union of individual soul and universal consciousness. This "Seal of Knowledge" stimulates the pituitary gland and endocrine system, sharpening your focus and clarity.
